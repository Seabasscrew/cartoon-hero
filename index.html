Nathan Drake

About Nathan Drake

Nathan Drake is the sheer definition of cool. He has the epitome of perfect sarcasm and a funny personality. He reminds me of Indiana Jones with the type of character that he is.

Nathan Drake and Indiana Jones side-by-side

Nathan Drake's characteristics

  1. He is a descendant of Sir Francis Drake.
  2. He never really had a family growing up.
  3. He is always about exploring new areas and finding different artifacts.
